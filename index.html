Saudi Kabsa

The spices and ingredients are collected together and some other flavors are added to them before we add the rice at the end

Recipe Overview

Why you’ll love it:

You must taste it to know

How long it takes:

45 minutes

Equipment you’ll need:

A large pot, a little oil, onions, spices, chicken or meat, what do you prefer? Water and in the end you have to add rice

Servings:

10

What You Need

  • Rice: There are many types of rice, choose the one you like best.
  • Onions: You have to add onions first to give a good flavor to the other ingredients,
  • spices There are a lot of options to add what do you prefer hot spices?.
  • chicken or meat : Then you have to add chicken or meat with vegetables if you want, add a little water and let the ingredients mix with each other.

    • After the water level becomes low, you have to add rice and wait for the result, can you do it?
